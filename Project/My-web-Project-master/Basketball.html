4 NEW COMMITS MAKE IT OFFICIAL FOR STALLIONS BASKETBALL

Brandon Stokes (6'0 Guard) Myron Wright (6'7 Forward) Colton Howard (6'8 Wing) and Earnest Matthews (5'9 Guard) have all officially signed to join the Stallions basketball team in the Fall of 2015. This will help the upper class be more competitive and help this program grow. Stokes, Wright and Howard all played at Conroe High School under Coach John Oliverio. Matthews played at Worthing High School and is excited for a fresh start here at NAU.
