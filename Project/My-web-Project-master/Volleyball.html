VOLLEYBALL NEWS

SEASON COMES TO AN END FOR OUR LADY STALLIONS


In the 2014-2015 Volleyball season, the Lady Stallions competed in the Houston Sports Club Conference. Playing against Univeristy of Houston, U of H Downtown, Lone Star College, and other colleges and helping to put the name of NAU out to other campuses.

With some of that it has been bringing interest to those who are interested to play for NAU and some have already planned to enroll for Fall 2015. Things are looking promising for this team and the future is becoming the present.
