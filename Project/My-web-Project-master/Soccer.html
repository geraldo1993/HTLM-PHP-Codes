STALLIONS SOCCER COME UP SHORT IN HARD FOUGHT 2-1 LOSS


The NAU Stallions Soccer team faced off against the Houston Dutch Lions, a semi-pro team in Conroe, in a friendly competition. Coach Rios brought out some of his prospects and newly signed players to see their potential in teamwork with current players. First half got to a slow start with a goal from the Dutch in the 15th minute of the game, but answered with a nice goal from Sherrif Umar in the 30th minute of the first half.

Second half was a back and forth battle but the Dutch found a weak spot and scored on the Stallions in the 53rd minute of the game. From there the Stallions tried hard to go for the tie but time was not on their side. Final score was 2-1 with the Houston Dutch winning. Coach Rios saw a lot of promise by some of his recruits and hopes to see more in future matches
